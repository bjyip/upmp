UPMP, your one stop castings from concept Development to finished product.

Established in 2009, United Precision Metal Product LLC.(UPMP) started as precision casting foundry using the “lost-wax” method. UPMP now has a annual sales over US$10M. Annual casting capacity: 10,000T. UPMP plant size over 30,000 sq., employee over 200. Our vertical integration of tooling development, casting, machining, heat treat specialize in ductile iron, grey iron, carbon steel, alloy steel, HLSA and stainless steel casting. We are proud to be a foundry with environmental awareness that choose silica sol for investment casting. Annual product over 10,000T used in automotive, ship manufacturing, high speed train, industrial equipment, mining, oil and medical application.

We are TS16949 certified in quality. Utilizing well accepted industry process quality standard and varies quality inspection equipment on site. Our quality lab is capable conducting varies chemical and mechanical testing to ensure product quality. Casting flow simulation, spectrograph, metallurgical microscope, Brinell, Vickers, Rockwell hardness test, tensile test, MPI, X-ray, CMM station and production functional test rigs.
